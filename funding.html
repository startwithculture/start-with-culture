Our funding

portfolio at one glance

Content Production Fund

To support solo artist and bands based in Africa in the field of music and video production.

To stay relevant musicians need to create constantly new music (album, singles). In times of social media, visual content has become the standard, especially in order to be competitive on the international market. Funding is available for creating music, videos, content for marketing of a music album or single, video EPK or showreel.

Mobility Fund

To support sustainable travel  for local tours, as well as to neighboring countries from the DAC list.

Concert fees are not covering all travel costs? With this fund we help with travel expenses for show case festivals or international tours.
