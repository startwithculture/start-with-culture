Funding for African Artists

October 12, 2021

A discussion panel about the important topic of FUNDING PROGRAMS FOR AFRICAN MUSICIANS presented by the German NGO Start With Culture gUG.

Close

Topics

Which funding programs exist.
The SITUATION due to the pandemic and how it reflects in the funding situation.
The current NEEDS of artists and how do these needs match with available programs.
Which new programs could arise and how could they be created and how would that impact the careers of artists?
