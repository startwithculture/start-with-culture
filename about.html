ABOUT US

START WITH CULTURE arises from the common belief that music and culture have the power to connect people regardless of their differences.
In 2020 during the global pandemic crisis, the founders of SWC felt the urgent need to put their experience and knowledge into a sustainable structure to raise awareness and create an effective network of people working together in the African music sector. They established the non-profit organization START WITH CULTURE to create positive change

  • through music and culture
  • through connecting professionals
  • through creating visions and strategies for and with African artists to build sustainable careers for them on a global scale and market

At the START WITH CULTURE organization, we live our lives by this principle of diversity, respect for other cultures, and the empowerment for artists to be able to create sustainable income and to raise their living standards.
